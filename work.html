IT Support

The work of IT support involves providing technical assistance and support to individuals or organizations experiencing issues with their computer systems, software, hardware, or other technology-related problems. IT support professionals are responsible for diagnosing and resolving issues to ensure the smooth functioning of technology and to minimize disruptions in daily operations.

Online Support

The work of an online support representative, also known as an online customer support representative or online help desk agent, involves providing assistance and resolving inquiries from customers or clients through online channels. These representatives typically communicate with customers via email, live chat, social media platforms, or online ticketing systems.

Junior Network/System Administrator

The work of a network/system administrator revolves around the management, maintenance, and security of an organization's computer network and IT systems. These professionals play a critical role in ensuring the smooth operation and efficiency of the organization's technology infrastructure.
